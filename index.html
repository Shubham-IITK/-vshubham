About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur, working under the supervision of Prof. Ankush Sharma and Prof. Prabodh Bajpai since August 2020. My doctoral research is funded by the MHRD Scholarship, Government of India.

My thesis is titled "Ancillary Service Provision using Distribution Network Resources for Grid Reliability and Stability." This explores how DERs, EVs, and active distribution networks can be coordinated to provide critical grid support services including primary frequency response, voltage regulation, and operational reserve.

I worked as a Visiting Scholar at Washington State University (WSU), Pullman, USA from June–August 2023 under the UI-ASSIST project, collaborating with Dr. Noel Schulz and Dr. Sanjeev Pannala on virtual storage bank concepts and energy management.

I actively contribute as a peer reviewer for ScienceDirect, RE&PQJ, RESEM (Bhopal) and TPEC (USA). I also served as a tutor for Introduction to Electrical Engineering and Electronics for three semesters at IIT Kanpur.

Research

My research lies at the intersection of power systems, smart grid technologies, and optimization. I investigate how distribution network resources can actively contribute to grid stability and ancillary service markets.

Research Areas
⚡
Ancillary Services from Distribution Networks
Operational reserve, voltage regulation, PFR and SFR from active distribution network resources.
🔋
DER Aggregation & Energy Management
Aggregating distributed energy resources for grid-level services; optimal scheduling of active distribution networks.
🚗
EV Integration & Virtual Storage Bank
EV integration in distribution networks; Virtual Storage Bank using EV/E-bus idle time for grid services.
🌐
TSO-DSO Co-Simulation
Transmission-Distribution co-simulation for real-time ancillary service provision and smart grid resilience.
📊
Power System Modeling & Load Flow
Distribution system modeling, load flow analysis and power quality using PSS/E, OpenDSS and MATLAB.
♻️
Renewable Integration & Optimization
Control strategies and optimization for integrating renewable energy sources and improving grid reliability.
